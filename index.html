Product Highlights

The Jowl SxS 5/8-inch recovery split shackle is designed for smaller off-road vehicles like SxS, ATV, and UTVs. Its patented split "J" design eliminates the need for tools or soft shackles, allowing fast, tool-free recovery without removing it from the bumper or hitch receiver.

With a classic bow shackle shape, it maintains a traditional look while incorporating modern functionality, including built-in pull angle stops for safe and efficient recoveries. It offers universal fit for 1.25-inch or 2.0-inch tow shackle receivers and features interchangeable colors to match vehicle aesthetics.

Constructed from 3.5T (7,000 LB WLL) forged carbon steel, it is built for durability, with rubber anti-chatter pads to prevent rattling on rough terrain. The shackle pin, secured by two Metric M14 cap screws, allows for easy installation and removal using a 1/4-inch socket wrench, with only one screw needing removal for quick attachment.

The Jowl SxS recovery shackle ensures strong, efficient, and hassle-free vehicle recovery, getting you back on the trail faster.
